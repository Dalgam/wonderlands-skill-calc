Dire Sacrifice

The Fatemaker sacrifices a portion of their Health to deal Dark Magic Ability Damage and apply Dark Magic Status Effects to all enemies nearby.

Dire Sacrifice deals Bonus Damage based on the amount of sacrificed Health.

Health Sacrificed: 40% of Current Health
Bonus Damage: 150% of Sacrificed Health
Cooldown: 25 seconds

Reaper of Bones

The Fatemaker is fully Healed, gains increased Leech Efficiency, and deals Bonus Dark Magic Damage, but their Health takes and ever-increasing amount of damage per second.

When the Fatemaker would die, they instead become Invulnerable. Reaper of Bones ends.

Action Skill Duration is based on current Health percentage and cannot be modified by other sources.

Bonus Dark Magic Damage: 20.0%
Leech Efficiency: 25.0%
Invulnerability: 3 seconds
Health Restoration: 20% of Maximum Health
Cooldown: 50 seconds
Demi-Lich Companion: The Fatemaker is accompanied by a floating Demi-Lich Companion that targets enemies at range and deals Dark Magic damage. Whenever the Fatemaker casts a Spell, the Demi-Lich will cast Hellish Blast, creating a homing projectile of the Spell's elemental type, dealing damage to nearby enemies on impact. Any increases to the Fatemaker's Damage also apply to their Companions.
